Chicken & Rice Bowl
Ingredients:
 - chicken
 - rice
 - soy sauce
 - oil
 - salt

Allergens:
 - soy

Steps:
 - Cook rice according to package instructions.
 - Season and pan-fry chicken until cooked through.
 - Mix chicken with soy sauce and serve over rice with steamed veggies.

Time: 25 minutes

Nutrition (rough estimate):
 - Calories: 451
 - Protein: 42g
 - Carbs: 52g
 - Fat: 7.3g
